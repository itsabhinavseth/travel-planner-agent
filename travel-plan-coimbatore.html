📅 Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Thursday, April 2

Morning: Fly from Bengaluru (BLR) to Coimbatore (CJB). Check into accommodation.
Afternoon: Lunch at Adyar Ananda Bhavan, Bharathiyar Road — iconic South Indian vegetarian restaurant. Explore the RS Puram neighbourhood.
Evening: Stroll through VOC Park & Zoo, Park Road, Central Zone — Coimbatore's historic park (open sunrise to sunset).

Day 2 — Friday, April 3

Morning: Visit Marudhamalai Temple — hilltop temple with panoramic views of Coimbatore, West Zone.
Afternoon: Lunch at Biggies Burger, NSR Road, Saibaba Colony — popular American-style burger joint with outdoor seating.
Evening: Explore Brookefields Mall and the Gandhipuram commercial district for shopping.

Day 3 — Saturday, April 4

Morning: Day trip to Siruvani Waterfalls — known for having some of the tastiest water in Asia, surrounded by lush forest.
Afternoon: Picnic lunch near Siruvani. Explore the trekking trails in the Western Ghats foothills.
Evening: Dinner at Adyar Ananda Bhavan, Mettupalayam Road — regional sweets and South Indian thali.

Day 4 — Sunday, April 5

Morning: Visit Gedee Car Museum, Avinashi Road — vintage automobile collection in Coimbatore.
Afternoon: Explore ISHA Yoga Center (Adiyogi Shiva statue) at the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains.
Evening: Catch a movie at Senthil Kumaran Theatre, Balaji Road, Gandhipuram — a local cultural experience.

Day 5 — Monday, April 6

Morning: Day trip to Ooty (Udhagamandalam) via the Nilgiri Mountain Railway — UNESCO World Heritage scenic train ride.
Afternoon: Visit Ooty Botanical Gardens and Ooty Lake. Lunch at a local Nilgiri restaurant.
Evening: Return to Coimbatore. Dinner at Adyar Ananda Bhavan, Sir Shanmugam Road, RS Puram.

Day 6 — Tuesday, April 7

Morning: Visit Perur Pateeswarar Temple — ancient Shiva temple with stunning Dravidian architecture, Perur.
Afternoon: Explore VOC Park & Zoo in detail — the zoological section with diverse wildlife. Lunch at a nearby eatery in Central Zone.
Evening: Walk through Town Hall area and explore local street food in Gandhipuram.

Day 7 — Wednesday, April 8

Morning: Day trip to Valparai — scenic hill station with tea plantations and misty mountains.
Afternoon: Visit Monkey Falls and Sholayar Dam en route. Lunch at a plantation café.
Evening: Return to Coimbatore. Relaxed dinner at a restaurant in Saibaba Colony.

Day 8 — Thursday, April 9

Morning: Visit Kovai Kutralam Falls (Siruvani area) — a refreshing waterfall experience in the Western Ghats.
Afternoon: Explore Coimbatore Wetlands — birdwatching at one of the important birding sites in Tamil Nadu.
Evening: Shopping at RS Puram — browse textiles, silk sarees, and local handicrafts.

Day 9 — Friday, April 10

Morning: Visit Dhyanalinga Temple at ISHA Foundation — a unique meditative space with no specific religious affiliation.
Afternoon: Explore Pollachi — a charming town known for coconut groves and the Anamalai Tiger Reserve entrance.
Evening: Farewell dinner at Adyar Ananda Bhavan, Bharathiyar Road — enjoy a grand South Indian feast.

Day 10 — Saturday, April 11

Morning: Sleep in. Late breakfast at accommodation. Visit any remaining spots in Gandhipuram or RS Puram.
Afternoon: Last-minute souvenir shopping — Coimbatore is famous for textiles and jewellery. Visit Saibaba Colony markets.
Evening: Pack and prepare for departure. Light dinner nearby.

Day 11 — Sunday, April 12

Morning: Check out. Head to Coimbatore International Airport (CJB).
Afternoon: Fly back to Bengaluru (BLR). Trip complete!
